"(Home)eostasis" is a short film based around the song 'View Between Villages'by Noah Kahan, and my own personal relationship to the song's message. It is a film about returning home to a place so full of memories that you no longer know how to feel about it.

"Cards Against Humility" is a short comedic film created for my Editing course at ISU, and follows the course of an unusual card game between two people which tests the boundaries of their comfort zones.

"Happy Together" is a short film based around the song 'Happy Together, following the mental spiral of a jealous girl secretly in love with her best friend when said friend gets a boyfriend. This project was created with classmates in my Filmmaking I course at ISU.

This short is a very simple project done for my Editing course at ISU which required us to create a short video using only pictures. My short tells the tale of two stuffed dinosaurs in the impending meteor crash.

This video is a project done for my Editing course at ISU which required us to create a short film which focused specifically on continuity editing. This film follows a young man, lost on his first day of class.
